FLORA

Overview

Flora is the new way to manage your plants and ensure they are always healthy and thriving. Through a mobile app, you receive detailed information about the status, specifics, and actions to take for each plant, all thanks to smart pots developed by specialists and equipped with various sensors necessary to keep your flora growing strong!
